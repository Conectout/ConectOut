Research Areas

Researching technologies across intelligence, computation and physical systems.

ConectOut investigates foundational technologies with an emphasis on understanding systems deeply, developing experimental prototypes and exploring architectures that can operate beyond conventional cloud-dependent computing models.

01 / AI

Sovereign Artificial Intelligence

Research into local and offline AI systems, retrieval architectures, knowledge systems and intelligent software capable of operating with reduced dependence on centralized cloud infrastructure.

02 / EMBEDDED

Embedded Systems

Experimental computing systems built around microcontrollers, constrained hardware, real-time processing and software designed to interact directly with physical environments.

03 / SIMULATION

Computational Simulation

Research into software engines for modeling physical systems, numerical computation and the simulation of complex interactions between computational and physical environments.

04 / AUTONOMY

Autonomous Systems

Exploration of perception, decision-making and control architectures for intelligent machines operating with increasing levels of autonomy.

05 / SYSTEMS

Experimental Operating Systems

Research into lightweight software architectures, hardware-software interaction and experimental operating environments for embedded and intelligent computing platforms.

06 / KNOWLEDGE

Intelligent Knowledge Systems

Development of retrieval, semantic search and retrieval-augmented generation architectures for building AI systems that reason over structured and unstructured knowledge.

Engineering Philosophy

Understand the system. Then engineer it.

First-Principles Engineering

Complex systems are approached by understanding their fundamental components rather than treating technology as an opaque abstraction.

Research Through Prototyping

Ideas become meaningful when they can be implemented, measured, tested and improved through real engineering work.

Local Intelligence

We explore systems capable of processing information locally, increasing technological independence, resilience and control over computing infrastructure.

Long-Term Technical Capability

ConectOut focuses on building knowledge, engineering capability and foundational technology rather than pursuing short-lived technology trends.

About ConectOut

Building technical capability for complex systems.

ConectOut is an independent technology research and development initiative exploring artificial intelligence, embedded computing, computational simulation and experimental software architectures.

Our work focuses on learning how complex technological systems operate, developing experimental prototypes and gradually building the engineering capability required to research more advanced intelligent and autonomous systems.

ConectOut is at an early stage of development. The projects presented here represent ongoing research, experiments and prototypes rather than claims of deployed commercial or operational systems.
